 Ce cours vise à forcer les étudiants à prendre en main les codes d'IA/ machine learning/Deep learning.

Artificial intelligence is one of the most trending domain today. The goal of this course is to make you work on different project on Artificial intelligence. You will have to handl the datasets, to build an AI model, and to find a way to train it, and to present it.
